The color palettes of Disney!

Colors are really important in animations because they often symbolize character traits or moods that the audiences can visually connect with. Especially in Disney films, colors are used to represent good vs. evil, character personalities or character growth. Let's explore how color makes our favorite Disney features so memorable!

Turning Red

Take Turning Red, a movie with so much spunk and bright energy! Through the usage of vivid colors, we can really follow Mei's arc and appreciate what she loves as well. Through colors, we learn about her culture, her emotions and her journey through adolescence.

Coco

Coco is one movie that really takes colors to a new level. It depicts the world of the dead as bright and energetic. Alongside the power of music, the vibrant oranges, glowing neons and warm color palette emphasize a sense of family and adventure.

Beauty and the Beast

Beauty and the Beast makes use of golds and blues to showcase the warmth Belle brings when she enters the dark, cold castle. Quite literally, she warms up the Beast's heart and shines as the heroine with her bright color palette. We can see the castle transform as the Beast is rejuvenated.

Princess and the Frog

The swampy color palette in Princess and the Frog and the vibrant parades in New Orleans sets the tone of the entire movie. The prevalent use of the color green represents multiple things: greed, ambition and nature itself.

Inside Out

Inside Out heavily uses color to convey each emotion in Riley's head! From the pastel, bubblegum palette of her childhood memories to the dark landscape of everything she forgets, Inside Out beautifully showcases the complex workings inside someone's brain.

Tangled

In Tangled, warm, glowy shades of yellow and gold represent goodness, like the healing aspects of Rapunzel's flowing locks, whereas the tower Rapunzel is trapped in is shrouded in shadows. The purple and gold palette is a beacon of hope and adventure, as they are the colors of the lantern-filled sky, Rapunzel's dress and more!
